📷 The Art of Visual Storytelling

Photography is the incredible art of freezing a split second in time, capturing emotions, stories, and perspectives that words often fail to describe. For students, photography is a powerful tool to observe the world closely, develop patience, and express their unique internal vision through light and shadow.

"You don't take a photograph, you make it. The heart and the mind are the true lens of the camera." — Ansel Adams
