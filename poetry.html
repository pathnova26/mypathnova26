🖋️ Wisdom from Mahakavi Kalidasa

Kalidasa, India’s greatest classical Sanskrit poet, transformed his destiny from an uneducated youth to a legendary scholar through sheer devotion to learning. His journey is the ultimate inspiration for every student: Knowledge can rewrite your fate.

"Look to this day: For it is life, the very life of life. For yesterday is but a dream, and tomorrow is only a vision. But today well-lived makes every yesterday a dream of happiness, and every tomorrow a vision of hope."
— (Attributed to Kalidasa's 'Salutation to the Dawn')
