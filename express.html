Why We Started PCB Express?

Most Biology students believe that MBBS is the only successful path after 12th. This massive misconception creates immense pressure and heartbreak. We started the PCB Express Series to shatter this myth. The world of healthcare, research, biotechnology, and allied sciences is vast. Our goal is to introduce you to 120+ high-growth alternative career paths, giving every biology student the clarity, direction, and opportunity to build an extraordinary future!
