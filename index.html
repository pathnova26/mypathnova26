Meet the Founder

Adity Kumari

Founder, PathNova

I started PathNova with a simple mission: to help students make informed decisions about their streams, careers, colleges, and future opportunities.

Today, many students feel pressured to follow only a few popular paths, especially competitive exams like JEE and NEET. Due to limited awareness of alternative opportunities, some students begin to believe that their entire future depends on a single exam. This mindset often creates unnecessary stress, anxiety, and hopelessness.

PathNova aims to change that by providing students with clear, accessible, and unbiased information about different career options, entrance exams, colleges, scholarships, government opportunities, and skill-based pathways.

At the same time, I believe that success is not limited to academics alone. Talents in sports, arts, music, dance, painting, innovation, and other creative fields deserve equal recognition and support. Through PathNova's "Rising Star" initiative, I hope to showcase young achievers and inspire students to pursue their passions with confidence.

"My vision is to build a platform where every student can discover opportunities beyond conventional paths, make informed choices, and find a future that matches their interests, strengths, and dreams."
