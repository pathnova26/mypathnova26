🏛️ Behind The Design: Sacred Geometry

The background of this page features the historic Garbhagriha (Sanctum Sanctorum) of the Shree Ram Janmabhoomi Mandir, Ayodhya, captured during its pristine construction phase.

Built in the traditional Nagara style architecture, every carved pillar and stone alignment on this wall represents the golden ratios, absolute symmetry, and mathematical precision of ancient Indian design—serving as the ultimate inspiration for modern graphic artists.
